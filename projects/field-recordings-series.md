# Field Recordings Series

## Archive
A collection of environmental recordings captured in various locations:

### Urban
- Train station ambience (Philadelphia, 2024)
- Construction site (Brooklyn, 2024)
- Traffic intersection (Manhattan, 2024)

### Rural
- Forest ambience (Pennsylvania, 2024)
- Stream/water (Catskills, 2024)
- Wind/open field (upstate NY, 2024)

## Equipment
- **Recorder:** Zoom H5
- **Microphone:** Rode NTG2 (shotgun)
- **Processing:** iZotope RX (noise reduction, spectral editing)

## Usage
These recordings are archived for use in future composition and sound design projects. Selected recordings may be released as standalone works.
